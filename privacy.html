Website information

This website is informational. If you contact the practice by email, you choose what information you share. Please avoid sending sensitive personal details by email.

Information you provide

If you email, you may provide your name, contact details, and general information about scheduling or your reason for seeking therapy. Email is not guaranteed to be secure, and it is not appropriate for emergencies.

Sharing

Information is not sold. It may be shared only as required by law or as needed to operate the practice (for example, email and website providers).

Therapy privacy

Privacy and confidentiality for psychotherapy services are governed by applicable laws and professional standards and are reviewed during intake. This page does not replace a formal Notice of Privacy Practices (if applicable).
