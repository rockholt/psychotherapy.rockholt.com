Approach

Therapy is a space to understand what you’re experiencing and build new ways of responding—internally and in relationships. The work is collaborative, paced to your comfort, and grounded in evidence-based practice.

Client-centered

Your goals guide the work. We’ll revisit them regularly and adjust as needed.

Skill building

Practical tools for emotional regulation, boundaries, and more helpful thinking patterns.

Meaning & context

We explore the “why” behind patterns—without judgment—so change can stick.

What sessions can feel like

  • A conversation that helps you name what’s happening
  • A place to practice new ways of responding to stress
  • A steady relationship where your experience is taken seriously
